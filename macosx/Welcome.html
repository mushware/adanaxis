Infernal Contractor II version 0.1.0

Take on the role of the software contractor and raise mayhem in this office simulation. This first stage sees you racing around the office against the clock in an attempt to convince people of your fitness. Please see the installed README file for more information.

Uninstalliing

To uninstall, remove the Infernal Contractor II directory by dragging it to the trash.

Known bugs

On machines without working 3D accelerators (like a beige G3/300MHz) the game will not work in fullscreen mode. If you have one of these, the game wil fade the screen a few times whilst attempting to select a mode, and then give up.
